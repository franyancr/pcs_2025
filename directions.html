Directions

The PCS 2025 Workshop will take place at two different locations on the University of New Mexico main campus in Albuquerque. On Thursday (09/18), the workshop will take place in room 1044 of the Centennial Engineering Center building, located at 300 Redondo W Dr. On Friday and Saturday (09/19-09/20), the workshop will take place in room 2540 (second floor) of the Physics & Astronomy and Interdisciplinary Science (PAIS) building, located at 210 Yale Blvd NE. Coffee breaks and lunches (Thursday & Friday) are included for workshop participants, and will be served  near the location of the talks.
